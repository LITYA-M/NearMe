koyambedu, Chennai: What It Is Famous For

Koyambedu is a neighbourhood in Chennai, India.
Situated in the western part of Chennai city, the Koyambedu area has become a major hub of activity in Chennai after the inauguration of the Koyambedu market in 1996

The Main Attraction:

koyambedu Market:
Koyambedu Wholesale Market Complex (KWMC) is one of Asia's largest perishable goods market complex located at Koyambedu, Chennai.
The market complex is spread over an area of 295 acres (1.19 km2).
Inaugurated in 1996, the complex consists of about 3,100 shops,[1] including more than 1,000 wholesale shops and 2,000 retail shops. Of these, 850 are fruit shops.
