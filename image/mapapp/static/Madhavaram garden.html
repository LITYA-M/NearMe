Madhavaram Botanical garden, Chennai: What It Is Famous For

Madhavaram Botanical Garden is a botanical garden in Chennai, India, set up by the horticulture department of the Government of Tamil Nadu.
The garden, the second botanical garden in Chennai after the Semmozhi Poonga, is the largest botanical garden in the city.

The Main Attraction:

Madhavaram Botanical Garden's main attractions include diverse themed gardens (medicinal, fruit, ornamental, cactus), a glasshouse, a lake with coracle/pedal boat rides, a children's play area, fountains, and an open-air theatre, offering a green escape with plant diversity, recreation, and learning opportunities in Chennai.
Visitors enjoy walking paths, photography, picnicking, bird watching, and exploring unique features like a bog garden and planned birthstar garden.
